About Us

Movement Disorders Summit

The Kuwait Movement Disorders Summit is a collaborative effort organized by Alpha Creative. We are dedicated to empowering individuals and families affected by movement disorders by fostering education, innovation, and a supportive community.

Our Mission:

. To provide a platform for healthcare professionals to share the latest advancements in research, diagnosis, and treatment of movement disorders.

. To empower patients and caregivers with knowledge and resources to manage their condition effectively and improve their quality of life.

. To foster collaboration among researchers, clinicians, patient advocacy groups, and industry partners to accelerate progress towards a cure for movement disorders.

Our Team :

The Kuwait Movement Disorders Summit is led by a dedicated team of professionals with extensive experience in the field. Our team members include:

  • Movement disorder specialists
  • Neurologists
  • Patient advocates
  • Healthcare industry representatives

We are passionate about making a difference in the lives of those affected by movement disorders. By bringing together leading experts and the movement disorders community, we aim to create a positive impact on the future of this field.

Join the Movement:

We invite you to join us at the Kuwait Movement Disorders Summit. Together, we can build a brighter future for individuals living with movement disorders.
